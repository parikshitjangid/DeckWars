# DeckWars × HeLa Labs Builder Guidelines

This document describes how **DeckWars** aligns with the [HeLa Labs Builder Guidelines & Participation Framework](https://helalabs.com).

---

## 1. About HeLa & DeckWars

**HeLa** is the Self-Native Layer 1 for Personalized AI (co-developed with A*STAR's IHPC). HeLa Labs is the builder community around the protocol.

**DeckWars** is a fully onchain Trading Card Game built **on HeLa**, using **HLUSD** for all in-game payments and rewards. It fits HeLa’s focus on payments, DAO tooling, and education.

## 5. Participation Guidelines (How We Comply)

- **Build in public:** Progress and learnings can be shared in HeLa Discord and Telegram.  
- **Collaborate:** We credit HeLa, HLUSD, and open-source dependencies (e.g. OpenZeppelin).  
- **Respect IP:** No misuse of third-party IP; attribution in README and docs.  
- **Code of conduct:** Respectful and inclusive in all community interactions.  
- **Ship with intention:** Real utility (playable TCG, HLUSD flows, quests, seasons).  
- **Document:** README, this file, and in-app copy document the project and flow.
